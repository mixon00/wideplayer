About

WidePlayer expands supported in-feed videos into a wider overlay while keeping the feed layout stable.

X and Mastodon are supported now. Bluesky and LinkedIn are planned.

Help

Where does WidePlayer work?

WidePlayer works on supported in-feed videos on X and Mastodon.

Why doesn’t it appear on every video?

Some layouts and embedded players are still unsupported. WidePlayer skips videos it cannot enhance safely.

What’s the difference between Auto and Manual mode?

Auto mode expands supported videos as you scroll. Manual mode leaves videos normal until you use the player control.

Does WidePlayer collect data?

No. The extension stores local settings on your device and does not collect or sell user data.
